# VS Code Ollama & WPBasic - Pricing Strategy

## Overview

**Combined Monetization Model**: Freemium + Credits + Enterprise

- **Free Tier**: Core features, limited completions
- **Paid Tiers**: Subscription-based with monthly credits
- **Pay-as-You-Go**: Additional credits ($0.01 per completion)
- **Enterprise**: Custom licensing for teams/organizations

---

## Credit System

### How Credits Work

1 completion request = 1 credit  
Pricing: $0.01 per credit (bulk discounts available)

**Bulk Discounts:**
- 100 credits: $1.00 ($0.01 ea)
- 500 credits: $4.50 ($0.009 ea) — 10% off
- 1,000 credits: $8.00 ($0.008 ea) — 20% off
- 5,000 credits: $35.00 ($0.007 ea) — 30% off
- 10,000 credits: $60.00 ($0.006 ea) — 40% off

**Pro Tip**: Monthly subscriptions include free credits (included above)

### Credit Usage

- **Completion request**: 1 credit
- **QA analysis**: 1 credit
- **Team member**: Each has own monthly allowance
- **Unused credits**: Roll over for 3 months, then expire
